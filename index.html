Our Story

From a small roastery
to a community ritual.

Ember & Roast was founded in 2018 by two lifelong coffee lovers who believed that the space between "good" and "extraordinary" is patience. We source our beans directly from independent farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — relationships built over years, not contracts.

Every roast profile is dialed in by hand. We don't chase trends; we chase flavour. Walk through our door and you'll find a room full of people who agree: coffee is worth slowing down for.
